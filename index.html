Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my wedding invitation design?

Our user-friendly design interface allows you to personalize every aspect of your invitation. Choose fonts, colors, and layouts that match your style. Our team is always available to help with any customization needs.

Can I include both Hebrew and English text?

Yes! All our designs support bilingual text, allowing you to seamlessly incorporate both Hebrew and English text while maintaining beautiful typography and layout.

How long does the design process take?

Most designs can be customized and finalized within 2-3 business days. Rush orders are available for time-sensitive events.

Do you offer matching sets for invitations?

Yes, we offer complete matching sets including response cards, direction cards, and thank you notes to maintain a cohesive design throughout your wedding stationery.

Can I see samples before ordering?

We offer digital proofs of your customized design before printing. Physical sample packs are also available for a small fee, which is credited towards your final order.

What is your printing and shipping timeline?

Standard printing takes 5-7 business days, with shipping times varying by location. Express printing and shipping options are available for urgent orders.

Do you offer calligraphy services?

Yes, we partner with professional calligraphers for both Hebrew and English addressing services. Digital calligraphy options are also available.

What paper options are available?

We offer a variety of premium papers including smooth, textured, and metallic finishes. All papers are carefully selected to enhance your chosen design.

Can I track my order?

Yes, all orders include tracking information. You can monitor your order status through our online portal or receive updates via email.

What is your return policy?

We stand behind our quality. If you're not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 7 days of receipt, and we'll work to make it right.
